Vegetable Fried Rice

A colorful mix of stir-fried vegetables and fluffy rice tossed with savory sauces. A quick, healthy, and delicious meal for any time of the day.

This recipe brings together the perfect balance of taste and simplicity. Fresh vegetables, fluffy rice, and savory sauces blend harmoniously to create a meal that’s both nourishing and satisfying. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out, this step-by-step recipe ensures flavorful results every time. Serve it hot for a quick lunch or dinner everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably leftover)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, capsicum, beans, peas, corn)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon chopped garlic
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Steps to cook a delicious Vegetable Fried Rice

  1. Prepare the rice
    2. If using fresh rice, spread it on a plate and let it cool completely. Cold rice works best for fried rice.
  2. Heat the oil
    3. In a large wok or frying pan, heat oil on high flame.
  3. Sauté the garlic
    4. Add chopped garlic and stir for 10–15 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Cook the vegetables
    5. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes until slightly tender but still crunchy.
  5. Add rice and sauces
    6. Add the cooked rice, soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Toss everything together quickly.
  6. Garnish and serve
  7. Sprinkle chopped spring onions over the top and serve hot.
