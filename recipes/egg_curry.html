Mac and Cheese

Boiled eggs simmered in a spiced, aromatic tomato-onion gravy. A hearty Indian favorite that pairs beautifully with rice or bread.

This recipe brings together the perfect balance of taste and simplicity. Hard-boiled eggs and aromatic spices blend harmoniously in a rich tomato-onion gravy, creating a dish that’s both hearty and satisfying. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out, this step-by-step recipe ensures delicious, authentic results every time. Serve it warm with rice or bread for a wholesome meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 boiled eggs
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 medium tomatoes (pureed or finely chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1 cup water

Steps to make delicious egg curry

  1. Prepare the eggs
    2. Lightly fry the boiled eggs in a bit of oil until golden. Set aside.
  2. Cook the onions
    3. In the same pan, add chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste
    4. Stir for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add tomatoes and spices
    5. Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Make the curry base
    6. Add water, bring to a boil, then gently add the fried eggs.
  6. Finish and serve
    7. Simmer for 5 minutes, sprinkle garam masala, garnish with coriander leaves, and serve hot.
