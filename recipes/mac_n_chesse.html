Mac and Cheese

A quick and flavorful dish perfect for busy weeknights, combining fresh ingredients with a burst of savory spices

This recipe brings together the perfect balance of taste and simplicity. Fresh vegetables and aromatic herbs blend harmoniously to create a meal that’s both nourishing and satisfying. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out, this step-by-step recipe ensures delicious results every time. Serve it warm with your favorite sides, and enjoy a wholesome dish that will leave everyone asking for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 200 g elbow macaroni (about 2 cups)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk (whole milk for creaminess)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (or a mix of cheddar & mozzarella)
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Optional: paprika, garlic powder, or mustard powder for extra flavor

Steps to make delicious mac and cheese

  1. Cook the pasta Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Add macaroni and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the roux (sauce base) In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Add the flour and whisk for 1–2 minutes to remove the raw flour taste.
  3. Add the milk Slowly pour in the milk while whisking to avoid lumps. Continue stirring until the sauce thickens (about 5–7 minutes).
  4. Add the cheese Lower the heat, then add shredded cheese. Stir until smooth and creamy.
  5. Season Add salt, pepper, and any optional spices.
  6. Combine pasta and sauce Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce. Stir until every piece is coated.
  7. (Optional) Bake for extra flavor Transfer to a baking dish, sprinkle extra cheese and breadcrumbs on top, and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15–20 minutes until golden and crispy
